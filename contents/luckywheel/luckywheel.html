What is this?

A fun, goofy game to help you get started working out or spice up your workout routine.

How does it work?

Click the red button to spin the wheel. Whatever exercise you get, click the Start timer button to run the 45-second timer. When the time is up, click the Spin again to get back to the spin wheel. Do as many times as you like. This means if you get burpee seven times in a row, do it anyway.

Why did you make it?

Getting started is always the hardest part of cultivating a new habit. We could all use a little help with gamification.

What is it not?

This is not about getting a six-pack abs in six weeks. Sometimes, however, doing something is still better than nothing at all. Enjoy
